---
title: 'ABOUT ZEBRA'
description: 'Learn more about Zebra, a creative agency that drives a global artistic movement.'
---

<Paragraph>
  ZEBRA IS A CREATIVE AGENCY, FOUNDED IN 2019 IN THE CITY OF TBILISI. WHAT STARTED AS A CLOTHING LINE EVOLVED INTO A MULTIFACETED PLATFORM THAT NOW STANDS AT THE FOREFRONT OF A GLOBAL ARTISTIC MOVEMENT.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph>
  OUR CLASSIC ZEBRA SHIRTS HAS TRANSFORMED INTO ICONIC MERCHANDISE. THE ZEBRA SHOP IS NOW A MARKETPLACE, FEATURING NOT ONLY ITS MERCH BUT ALSO BRANCH BRANDS AND COLLABORATIONS.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph>
  FOUNDED IN THE HEART OF TBILISI, ZEBRA ESTABLISHED ITS ROOTS AS A CLOTHING LINE, MAKING AN INDELIBLE MARK AT THE 4GB ELECTRONIC MUSIC FESTIVAL IN GEORGIA. THE BRAND'S EVOLUTION EXTENDED BEYOND FASHION, ENCOMPASSING PERFORMANCES AT WORKSHOPS, FESTIVALS, LOCAL EVENTS AND DIFFERENT ORGANIZATIONS.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph>
  ZEBRA IS NOT JUST A BRAND; IT'S A DRIVING FORCE BEHIND AN ARTISTIC MOVEMENT. FROM ITS INCEPTION, THE AGENCY HAS BEEN ON A MISSION TO AMPLIFY THE VOICES OF CREATIVE MINDS WORLDWIDE, PIONEERING CROSS-BORDER TALENT EXCHANGES.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph>
  AS A COMPREHENSIVE CREATIVE AGENCY, WE OFFER MORE THAN JUST FASHION AND MUSIC. WITH A FOCUS ON VIDEOGRAPHY, INCLUDING MUSIC VIDEOS AND SHORT FILMS, THE AGENCY'S WEBSITE SERVES AS A CENTRAL HUB TO CONNECT WITH ZEBRA'S VIDEOGRAPHERS AND PRODUCTION TEAM.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph>
  ZEBRA ENGAGES WITH INTERNATIONAL ARTISTS, CURATING EVENTS THAT BRING DIVERSE GLOBAL TALENT TO LOCAL STAGES. THIS COMMITMENT ENRICHES THE CULTURAL FABRIC BY BLENDING UNIQUE SOUNDS AND EXPRESSIONS FROM AROUND THE WORLD.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph>
  COLLABORATING WITH EVENT ORGANIZERS, MUSIC FESTIVALS, AND CULTURAL INSTITUTIONS GLOBALLY, ZEBRA ACTIVELY SECURES PERFORMANCE OPPORTUNITIES FOR LOCAL TALENTS ON THE INTERNATIONAL STAGE. THE AGENCY'S COMMITMENT TO FOSTERING ARTISTIC GROWTH ENSURES EXPOSURE FOR ITS ARTISTS ON A TRULY GLOBAL SCALE.
</Paragraph>
